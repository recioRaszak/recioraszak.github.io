[1] About / Bio

Badalona -Barcelona, Spain- 1976.

With training and vocation as a creative graphic designer, I have focused most of my career on the development of interactive products, mostly for the web. In the last decade I have been working both in design and development, although more in the second, with WordPress as a content management platform, among others.

Currently I'm working on a personal e-Commerce project related to building set toys and collectibles.

I also have a funny blog about WordPress development with tips,articles and tutorials called CachitosWP, take a look at it!

I consider me an open and outgoing person, with a lot of hobbies and passions. I love comic books, pop culture, surfskate and lately I've been hitting Lego again.
